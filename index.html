Festa Junina WELCOME TO BRAZIL

What is Festa Junina?

Festa Junina is a traditional Brazilian folk celebration held throughout the country during the month of June. Inspired by Catholic saints’ festivals (Saint John, Saint Anthony, and Saint Peter), it brings together typical foods, quadrilha dances, games, regional music, and lots of joy.

The decorations are colorful, with pennant flags, balloons, stalls, and a rural atmosphere that recalls the Brazilian countryside. It’s a time for gathering, dancing, bonfires, and vibrant culture.

Experience the flavors and traditions: welcome to Festa Junina!
